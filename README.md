## Idea

This simple social media platform is supposed to fulfill all your cookie needs by sharing pictures of your best cookies 
with other cookie lovers all around the world. 

With Cookiegram you can...
- create a Cookiegram account
- share pictures of cookies
- comment on pictures of other cookiers (that's the official name of Cookiegram users) 
- give cookies (a better form of 'likes') to cookie pictures you like or you think deserve one
